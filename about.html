About Me

Hey, I'm Raine and I am the designer/maker behind Hookedonraine. I am based in Trinidad and Tobago, working from my home studio. Hookedonraine is a one-woman brand trying to do its bit in the fashion revolution.
Crochet is my passion and I love to make fun, colourful clothing and accessories. At Hookedonraine, I am passionate about the artistry and joy of crochet. Founded with a love for creating beautiful, handcrafted pieces, I specialize in handmade hats, bags, clothing and accessories.
When you choose Hookedonraine, you're not just purchasing a product; you're investing in a piece of artistry. Whether you're looking for a custom bag or a unique gift for a loved one, I am dedicated to creating something special just for you. I value your trust and strive to exceed your expectations with every creation.
Thank you to all my customers for supporting my passion and allowing me to do what I love!
